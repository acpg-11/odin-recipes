Teriyaki Ground Beef

A quick and easy classic

Teriyaki ground beef is a dish that everybody seems to love. It just hits the spot. Fortunately, it is not only healthy, but quick and esy to make.

Ingredients:

  • ground beef
  • white onion
  • minced garlic
  • carrots/broccoli
  • teriyaki sauce
  • brown sugar
  • red pepper flakes
  • white rice
  • cooking oil

Steps:

  1. star a pot of rice
  2. place oil into a frying pan and heat to medium
  3. place chopped onions into the pot and simmer for 2-3 minutes
  4. place ground beef into pot and cook until light brown
  5. place teriyaki sauce and brown sugar into pan, along with vegetables
  6. cook until sauce becomes desired texture
  7. place over cooked white rice
  8. enjoy
